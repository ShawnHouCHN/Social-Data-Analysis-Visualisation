Assignment 2A: One scatter plot and two datasets

Below is a data visualization of Prostitution and Vehicle Theft in San Francisco in 2003 and 2015. Each bubble represent a SFPD district. The size of the bubble corresponds to the total amount of crimes in that district that year (The total number is also presented as legend to the right). The coordinates of the bubbles are the amount of prostitution(X axis) and vehicle theft(Y axis) respectively. You can click the two navigation tabs below to transit between plots

  • 2003
  • 2015

Prostitution & Vehicle Theft 2003

Prostitution was centered in Mission, Nothern and Tenderloin in 2003 while vehicle theft majorly happened in Ingleside, Bayview and Taraval. Generally, vehicle theft case occured much more than prostitution.

Prostitution & Vehicle Theft 2015

´The trend was same in 2015. vehicle theft cases happened much more times and more dispersive than prostitution. Besides, the top 3 most occurred district for prostitution is Mission, Taraval and Southern while it is Ingleside, Mission and Bayview for vehicle theft cases.


Notice: The axes are kept constant to make it easier to compare the values of the two years. With the constant axes it is easy to see that the general amount of the two focus crimes has declined.
